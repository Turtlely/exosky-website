Welcome to Exosky!

This website allows you to explore the night sky from the perspective of any of the 5000+ exoplanets discovered.

Here's how to use the site:

  • Enter the name of an exoplanet in the left pane
  • Navigate the sky by clicking and moving with your cursor.
  • Shift + Left-click on stars to see their detailed information.
  • Control + left-click multiple stars to create your own constellations!

Note: This project runs on my personal computer, processing nearly 2 billion stars per active user! As a result, it may take up to 10 seconds for all stars to fully render. Please be patient, I promise that we are working hard on your request behind the scenes!
